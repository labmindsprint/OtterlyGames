How to Learn Division Facts

Division is essentially multiplication in reverse. If your child knows that 6 √ó 7 = 42, then they also know that 42 √∑ 7 = 6. Building division fluency therefore starts with strong times-table knowledge.

Use the Study mode to read through all 12 facts for a divisor, then switch to Practice mode and type the answers from memory. Start with √∑2, √∑5 and √∑10 before tackling harder divisors.

Tips for Parents

Keep sessions short ‚Äî five minutes of focused practice is more effective than a 30-minute slog. Praise effort and improvement rather than just correct answers.
