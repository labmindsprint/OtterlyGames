A Mom Who Gets It

It started with a struggle every parent knows. My son was in tears over reading a clock. Multiplication felt like a punishment. And I was drowning in screen time guilt — handing them a tablet just to get 20 minutes of peace, knowing they were learning nothing.

So I built what I wished existed: games that are genuinely fun and genuinely educational. No dark patterns. No data harvesting. No manipulative monetization. Just honest games made with love, designed to help kids learn without realizing it.

Every game is COPPA certified, collects zero personal data, and was tested by real kids (including my own very honest critic).
